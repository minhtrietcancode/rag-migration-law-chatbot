1  Short title
This Act may be cited as the Migration Act 1958.
  
2  Commencement
  
The several Parts of this Act shall come into operation on such 
dates as are respectively fixed by Proclamation.
3  Repeal and savings
(1) The Acts specified in the Schedule to this Act are repealed.
(2) Section 9 of the War Precautions Act Repeal Act 1920-1955 and 
the heading to that section, and the Schedule to that Act, are 
repealed.
(3) The War Precautions Act Repeal Act 1920-1955, as amended by 
this section, may be cited as the War Precautions Act Repeal Act 
1920-1958.
