120  Certain information must be given to visa holder
(1) In this section, relevant information means information (other than 
non-disclosable information) that the Minister considers:
(a) would be the reason, or a part of the reason, for cancelling a 
visa; and
(b) is specifically about the holder or another person and is not 
just about a class of persons of which the holder or other 
person is a member; and
(c) was not given by the holder; and
(d) was not disclosed to the holder in the notification under 
section 119.
(2) The Minister must by written notice:
(a) give particulars of the relevant information to the holder; and
(b) set out why it is relevant to the cancellation; and
(c) invite the holder to comment on it.
(3) The notice under subsection (2) must be given in the prescribed 
way.
