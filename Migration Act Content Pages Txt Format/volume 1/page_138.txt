Requirement to fit a monitoring device etc.
(2) A person commits an offence if:
(a) the person holds a Subclass 070 (Bridging (Removal 
Pending)) visa; and
(b) the visa is subject to a condition requiring the person to allow 
an authorised officer to fit, install, repair or remove:
(i) the person’s monitoring device; or
(ii) any related monitoring equipment for the person’s 
monitoring device; and
(c) the person fails to comply with the requirement of the 
condition.
Penalty: 5 years imprisonment or 300 penalty units, or both.
Requirements relating to good working order of monitoring device 
etc.
(3) A person commits an offence if:
(a) the person holds a Subclass 070 (Bridging (Removal 
Pending)) visa; and
(b) the visa is subject to a condition requiring the person to take 
any specified steps, and any other reasonable steps, to ensure 
that the following remain in good working order:
(i) the person’s monitoring device;
(ii) any related monitoring equipment for the person’s 
monitoring device; and
(c) the person fails to comply with the requirement of the 
condition.
Penalty: 5 years imprisonment or 300 penalty units, or both.
