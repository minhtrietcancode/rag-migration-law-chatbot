37  Bridging visas
There are classes of temporary visas, to be known as bridging 
visas, to be granted under Subdivision AF.
37A  Temporary safe haven visas
(1) There is a class of temporary visas to travel to, enter and remain in 
Australia, to be known as temporary safe haven visas.
Note: A temporary safe haven visa is granted to a person to give the person 
temporary safe haven in Australia.
(2) The Minister may, by notice in the Gazette, extend the visa period 
of a temporary safe haven visa so that the visa ceases to be in effect 
on the day specified in the notice.
(3) The Minister may, by notice in the Gazette, shorten the visa period 
of a temporary safe haven visa so that the visa ceases to be in effect 
on the day specified in the notice if, in the Minister’s opinion, 
temporary safe haven in Australia is no longer necessary for the 
holder of the visa because of changes of a fundamental, durable 
and stable nature in the country concerned.
