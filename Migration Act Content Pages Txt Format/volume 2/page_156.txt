369  Identifying information relating to reviewable protection 
decisions not to be published
(1) This section applies to reviewable protection decisions.
(2) The ART must not publish information under section 113 of the 
ART Act which may identify:
(a) an applicant for review of a decision; or
(b) any relative or other dependant of an applicant for review of 
a decision.
